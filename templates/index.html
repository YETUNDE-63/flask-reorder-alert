Reorder Alert Prediction

This tool predicts whether an item needs to be reordered based on the following inputs:

  • Opening Stock Qty: Stock quantity at the beginning of the period
  • Closing Stock Qty: Stock remaining at the end of the period
  • Stock Replenished: Quantity of stock added during the period (0 or 1)

Note: Item Name is for your reference only and is not used in the prediction model.
